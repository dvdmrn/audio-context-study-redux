Welcome!

In this experiment, you will be asked to evaluate footage of people having a conversation. A visualization of a speaker's acoustic environment may be employed. The study will be broken into two parts:
  • Rating task: You will be asked to rate a particular speaker's emotional state among a number of dimensions.
  • Environment task: You will be asked to determine what environment the speaker is situated in based off audio visualizations.
At the end of each stage, you will be prompted to download your data. Your data will be a small text file (.json). At the conclusion of the study, you will be given a google form to upload your data.

Audio is a cruicial component of this study, so please make sure you have headphones or speakers enabled.
