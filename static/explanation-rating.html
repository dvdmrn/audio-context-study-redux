Rating task

In this experiment you will be asked to rate the emotions of someone speaking.

You will rate the degree to which certain emotion words apply to the speaker from 1-5

A rating of 1 means it does not apply at all. A rating of 5 means it extremely applies.
For example, if someone is crying because their dog died, you may imagine they have a sadness rating of 5 and a happiness rating of 1. But if someone is crying because their child is leaving for college, you may imagine a sadness rating of 3 and happiness rating of 3.
